About

Hello new user, I am Srikar and welcome to SudokU: the customizable Sudoku webapp for any occasion.
Currently, the theme is simplistic grey, but customizations appear upon request.
You may play with a random board or upload your own custom game as a list of digits, row by row.
There is a solve feature which implements a backtracking algorithm and can solve any board.
Feel free to shoot me an email down below for any theme requests you have!
